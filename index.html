About me

From patient care to marketing — carefully, deliberately.

My first career was in healthcare. I earned a Bachelor of Science in Respiratory Therapy and worked in quality assurance and administration — environments where accuracy isn't optional, documentation matters, and every process has to hold up under scrutiny.

That discipline is exactly what I now bring to marketing. I'm completing a Business Administration – Marketing diploma at NAIT in Edmonton, and I've been putting it to work on real projects: building a brand and website for a massage therapy clinic, creating content, editing photo and video, and designing AI automations that save small businesses hours of repetitive work every week.

I'm the person who shows up prepared, keeps the spreadsheet clean, answers the email the same day, and quietly makes the whole operation run smoother. If you need someone who can coordinate a campaign and build the tracker for it — that's me.
